How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 9–18 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (May–October). Popular dates can book out quickly.

Can you learn our first dance song?

Absolutely! We’re happy to learn one special request (such as your first dance) free of charge. Additional custom songs can usually be arranged with advance notice.

What happens during your breaks?

We provide a professional DJ-style playlist between live sets to keep the energy flowing. Guests are welcome to make requests from our playlist.

How long do you perform?

A typical package includes:
2 x 60-minute sets or 3 x 40-minute sets
Background music between sets
Late-night DJ option (if selected)
We can tailor timing to your schedule.

Are you insured?

Yes, we carry full public liability insurance and all equipment is PAT tested (where required).

What is your pricing?

Pricing depends on:
Band size
Location
Performance times
Additional services (ceremony music, DJ, lighting upgrades)
Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your wedding.

What happens if a band member is unavailable?

We have a trusted network of professional musicians. In the unlikely event of illness, we ensure a seamless replacement of equal quality.
